A beginner's guide to thinking algorithmically and competing with confidence.

Competitive programming is the sport of solving well-defined algorithmic problems under time pressure. It's part puzzle, part math, and part engineering — and it's one of the fastest ways to sharpen your problem-solving instincts as a developer.

The core loop is simple: read a problem, design an algorithm, code it, and submit. But behind that simplicity lies a rich world of data structures, graph theory, dynamic programming, and more. Each problem you solve teaches you a new mental model you can apply forever.
